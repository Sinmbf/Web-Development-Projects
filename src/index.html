Popular Treks In Nepal

Annapurna Base Camp

Annapurna Circuit Trek, a classic and famous trek, that takes you through some of the most diverse landscapes. It is a popular trekking route that encircles the Annapurna massif, you'll pass through lush green forests, terraced fields, and traditional villages.

Langtang Valley

Langtang Valley Trek is a relatively short trek that offers a wonderful blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences. It is one of Nepal's best treks. The trails take you through Langtang National Park, known for its diverse flora and fauna.

Everest Base Camp

The Everest Base Camp Trek is the ultimate adventure for trekkers and is considered, one of the best treks in the world. Some people even consider this EBC trek to be the most beautiful hike in Nepal.

A Little Bit About Nepal Eco-Tourism

On 27th February 2013, the Nepalese government approved the establishment of the Nepal Ecotourism Development Board. The Minister of Forests and Soil Conservation was designated as the chairman of the board.

In 2023, Nepal's Travel & Tourism sector played a significant role in the national economy, contributing NPR 14,722.3 billion, which accounted for 6.6% of the GDP. This sector has consistently shown robust growth, with a forecasted annual increase of 7.9% over the next decade.

The tourism industry's steady expansion highlights Nepal's appeal as a premier destination for natural beauty, adventure activities, and cultural heritage. From trekking in the Himalayas, including Mount Everest, to exploring UNESCO World Heritage sites like Kathmandu Valley, Nepal remains a top choice for global travelers.
